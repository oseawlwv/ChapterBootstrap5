Pictures Wanted!

We are looking for photos of classified staff on the job! Any photos you would like to submit can be sent to davissot@wlwv.k12.or.us. If we choose your photo to feature on our website or a future newsletter, you will be rewarded with a $10 gift card! When emailing photos that you want to submit, please include the name(s) of staff members in the photo. We will want to make sure we have their permission to use the photo. Also keep in mind that we love students, but these photos should feature adults only. If you are interested in contributing content for our website or have suggestions for information you would like to access there, please send feedback to secretary@osea-wlwv.com.

Sick Leave Bank

Are you a 10-month employee who exceeded your paid leave amount (sick/personal) due to your own sickness and/or that of those you care for, but you did not have a long enough absence to utilize the sick leave bank to cover that time? If this has happened to you, please reach out to secretary@osea-wlwv.com. We are looking for more information about staff who are being impacted in this way so we can find ways to better support them in the future. *The sick leave bank is a resource available to classified staff who opt-in to the program. For more information, see page 20 of our contract (or the 21st page of the pdf).

Para Shifts

Thank you to the library paraeducators who responded to our request for more information! Your help is greatly appreciated as we advocate for 6-hour library and online program paras to have the option of an additional hour. It is also helpful as we engage in discussions about the way the overall paraeducator classification and sub-classifications are handled. If you are a paraeducator who has not yet spoken to your supervisor about your status/schedule in the fall, we encourage you to do so before leaving for the summer.
