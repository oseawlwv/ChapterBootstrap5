Chapter 102 Voting

(11/13/2025) All members in good standing can make their voice heard at Chapter ballot questions held throughout the year.

Elections have been conducted online since COVID until this year, when the OSEA state leadership said we could no longer ballot online until we updated our constitution to specifically allow it. The Constitution Committee has since given our governing manuals a timely overhaul, combining constitution and bylaws into a single document. Once the new constitution is approved by the state organization and ratified by the members, we will return to online voting using the state approved vendor, Election Monkey. When ballot issues occur, advance notice will be given and members will receive links to vote in their email.

If you are a member and do not receive a link, please contact a member of the executive committee for verification and a paper ballot.

Until the new constitution is ratified, members will be alerted by email of upcoming votes, and where and when paper ballots may be cast in person.
