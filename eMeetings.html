Attending meetings remotely

Chapter 102 uses Zoom to allow members who can't physically attend to still participate and vote in regular Chapter meetings.
Zoom offers apps for both Android and iPhone devices, as well as extensions for Chrome, Firefox and Safari for viewing on computers. An account isn't necessary to join a meeting.

Before each event, a link will be emailed to the membership which will connect you to the meeting. It will prompt you to download the client first if you haven't already done so.
